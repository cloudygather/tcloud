About

Digital transformation of enterprise

The plan handled by Office of the Ministry of Economic Affairs gather various cloud digital tools to guide enterprises to choose the appropriate product. From July 1, 2021, provide online application apply for digital points. According to the plan, the subsidy can be up to NT 30,000 to encourage and ease the burden of enterprise.

Subsidy

Provide 80% subsidies

Each enterprise can obtain 30,000 digital points through online application and identity verification, and then when the enterprise purchases cloud solutions on the official platform of the event, it will pay according to self-pay and subsidy discount ratio, and the subsidy can be up to NT 30,000.
